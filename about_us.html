BCIT Comp 1950 Site Redesign Meta Site

This redesign meta site describes the proposal for how we will be redesigning the BCIT Comp 1950 website. This intro page includes the list of the team members and what each did. It has a link to the Proto-site that has some of the pages mocked up, including: dashboard/home, about and lectures. A Sitemap is included and a Rationale page. The Rational page will describe why we chose to build the site in the way that we proposed.
